The SEAI (Sustainable Energy Authority of Ireland) manages the Building Energy Rating (BER) assessments nationally.
Houses with a BER of A or B are highly energy efficient and typically have substantially lower heating bills and fewer carbon emissions. On average, upgrading from an E or F rating to an A or B rating can reduce annual heating costs by over €1,400 per year.
The National Retrofit Plan commits to upgrading 500,000 homes to a BER of B2 or better by 2030, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and addressing energy poverty.
Grants are available through SEAI, including individual energy upgrade grants, one-stop-shop services, and fully funded energy upgrades for eligible homes.
