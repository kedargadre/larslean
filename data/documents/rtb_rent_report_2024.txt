The Residential Tenancies Board (RTB) Rent Index for Q4 2023 shows that rents for new tenancies nationally increased by 8.5% compared with Q4 2022.
The standardised average rent for new tenancies in Q4 2023 was €1,595 per month.
In Dublin, the standardised rent for new tenancies increased by 6.0% year-on-year to €2,060.
Outside Dublin, rents for new tenancies increased by 10.5% year-on-year to €1,280.
Cork showed the highest rent growth amongst the regional cities, driven by lack of supply and sustained demand from local employment. There is an active shortage of 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom rental accommodations, heavily straining affordability for single households.
